Global Leaders Gather for Climate Change Summit Amid Rising Concerns

World leaders convened in Paris for an emergency climate summit as new data shows accelerating ice melt in polar regions. The conference aims to establish more aggressive carbon reduction targets and funding for developing nations.

Key discussions include the implementation of carbon pricing mechanisms, investment in renewable energy infrastructure, and strategies to protect vulnerable coastal communities from rising sea levels.
